Healthcare access & preventable hospitalizations

County-level modeling · Health equity

Built county-level models connecting primary care access, insurance coverage, and socioeconomic factors to preventable hospital stays, using County Health Rankings data and BRFSS-derived indicators.

  • Random Forests for variable importance.
  • Race-stratified Negative Binomial models with population offsets.
  • Choropleth maps and effect plots for policy audiences.

Energy & environmental policy

Petroleum markets · Price dispersion

Supported an environmental economics project studying Ghana’s downstream petroleum deregulation, focusing on price dispersion, tax pass-through, and market structure.

  • R pipelines for ingesting and cleaning 50K+ record pricing datasets.
  • Time-series summaries for policy reports and memos.

Methodology & simulation

Robustness studies · Teaching-focused work
