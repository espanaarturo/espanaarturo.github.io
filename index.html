Hello, and welcome to my website! I’m Arturo Espana, a senior at UC Santa Barbara, double-majoring in Statistics & Data Science and Economics. As a first-generation Mexican American student, I am passionate about using data to understand real-world problems and develop practical solutions in healthcare, public policy, and financial markets.

Outside of data science, I enjoy watching sports, playing poker, weightlifting, and gaming.
