Health & public policy

Projects focused on healthcare access, health equity, and public programs.

County-Level Risk Analysis of Healthcare Access and Racial Disparities in Preventable Hospitalizations

Random Forests · Negative Binomial regression · County Health Rankings

Lead author on a race-stratified modeling study using County Health Rankings and related data to examine how primary care access, insurance coverage, and socioeconomic conditions relate to preventable hospital stays across U.S. counties.

  • Fitted Random Forest models to identify key predictors by racial composition of counties.
  • Estimated Negative Binomial regressions with population offsets to obtain interpretable incidence rate ratios.
  • Produced maps and effect plots to communicate results to non-technical health policy audiences.

Modeling Preventable Hospital Stays at UnitedHealth Group

Industry data science internship · Risk modeling

Built regression and tree-based models on data from 3,000+ U.S. counties to explore the relationship between healthcare access, demographics, and preventable hospitalizations in a payer context.

  • Cleaned and merged multiple large administrative and public datasets into analysis-ready tables.
  • Evaluated model performance and interpretability for use in internal risk analytics.

Energy & environmental markets

Data pipelines and analysis for energy and environmental policy questions.

Ghana Petroleum Deregulation Analysis

Time-series · Price dispersion · Policy analytics

Contributed to an environmental economics project studying Ghana’s downstream petroleum deregulation, focusing on how reforms affected retail fuel price dispersion and tax pass-through.

  • Developed R pipelines to ingest, clean, and validate large petroleum pricing datasets.
  • Constructed station-level and region-level summaries for use in policy memos and presentations.

Spatial Housing Market Modeling

Spatial econometrics · California Central Coast

Built an end-to-end spatial ETL and modeling pipeline in R to join Census data, housing attributes, and geographic covariates in order to model neighborhood-level home values.

  • Used sf, spdep, and spatialreg to construct spatial weights and estimate spatial lag/error models.
  • Created diagnostic plots and maps to compare OLS to spatial regression approaches.

Robustness of ANOVA vs. Permutation Tests

Simulation study · Non-parametric inference

Simulation-based comparison of classical ANOVA and permutation tests under varying levels of normality and homoskedasticity violations, aimed at understanding when non-parametric approaches offer practical advantages.

  • Implemented simulation loops in R with reproducible code and clear visual summaries.
  • Produced a teaching-oriented technical report summarizing when each method is preferable.
